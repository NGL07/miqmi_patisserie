About Us

Our Story

Every great recipe has a story. Ours began years ago with a genuine passion for creating exceptional food. For many years, we welcomed our guests at Miqmi Cafe & Restaurant with dishes and desserts prepared fresh every day using only the finest ingredients. Throughout our journey, one principle has never changed: "We never serve anything we wouldn't proudly eat ourselves." Guided by this philosophy, every item leaving our kitchen has been crafted with premium ingredients, careful attention to detail, and genuine passion. Over time, our desserts became the signature of Miqmi. Handcrafted from our own recipes, they earned a special place in the hearts of our guests. Through word of mouth, recommendations from friends, and loyal customers who returned time and time again, Miqmi grew into a community built on trust, quality, and unforgettable flavors. Today, that story begins a new chapter. Miqmi Patisserie is our next journey—bringing back the desserts our customers have missed most while introducing many exciting new creations. As we prepare to reopen our café, we're delighted to reunite you with your favorite flavors through our online patisserie. Every order is prepared fresh, made to order, and carefully crafted to ensure it reaches you at its very best. Whether you're choosing one of our signature desserts, a bespoke celebration cake, or a custom creation for a special occasion, every order is made with the same care, precision, and excitement as the very first. Alongside our desserts, we continue to prepare a carefully selected range of savory favorites—from buttery croissants and freshly baked pastries to traditional börek, meze, and more. We also welcome custom orders for private events, celebrations, and special occasions. Today, we proudly bring together more than a decade of experience in gastronomy, a loyal community that has grown with us over the years, and the same passion that inspired us from the beginning. Miqmi Patisserie is more than a dessert brand. It is where passion, craftsmanship, and the joy of sharing come together. Because now, just as on day one, we still believe: The finest recipes begin with the finest ingredients—and are made with love.
